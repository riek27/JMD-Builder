Who We Are

South Sudan’s premier integrate services company

General Integrate Services Company Ltd (GIS CO. LTD) is a leading South Sudanese contracting and supply company. Headquartered in Juba, we deliver high-quality engineering, construction, trading, and technical services across the nation.

We specialize in civil and structural works, import/export, general supply, and turnkey construction solutions. Our core strengths are built on integrity, quality, innovation, and professionalism. With skilled manpower and modern equipment, we execute projects of any size and complexity — from residential buildings to bridges and mining operations.
