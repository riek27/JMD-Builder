Featured: Nile Road Construction

Location: Juba – Nimule, South Sudan

Duration: 2023–2024

This 120 km tarmac road connects Juba to the Ugandan border, facilitating trade and transport. Our scope included earthworks, drainage, asphalt paving, and bridge construction over the Ayi river. The project was delivered 3 months ahead of schedule.
