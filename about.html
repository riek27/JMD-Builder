About GIS CO. LTD

Integrating expertise for South Sudan’s development

Our Story

General Integrate Services Company Ltd (GIS CO. LTD) is a leading South Sudanese contracting and supply company, established to deliver high‑quality engineering, construction, trading, and technical services across the nation.

Our head office is located at Northleaf Building, No. 28, next to Former KCB, Munuki Hai Kuwait, Bilpam Road, Juba, Central Equatoria State. Since our inception, we have specialized in civil and structural works, import/export, general supply, and turnkey construction solutions for commercial and residential projects.

Our core strengths are built on integrity, quality, innovation, and professionalism. With the capacity to mobilize skilled manpower and modern equipment, we execute projects of any size and complexity — from site clearing to bridges, mining, and medical supply.

Our Objective

Our vision: To become a world‑class engineering and construction company providing efficient, affordable, sustainable, and cost‑effective services of the highest quality standards.

Our mission: To deliver reliable, innovative, and high‑quality engineering, construction, and supply solutions while maintaining strong customer relationships and contributing to the development of South Sudan.

We place our customers at the heart of our business. By ensuring efficient, dependable services, we allow clients to focus on their core operations. Customer satisfaction remains our top priority — always.

Our Strength: People & Machinery

Skilled manpower and modern equipment for any challenge

GIS CO. LTD brings together experienced engineers, project managers, and technical specialists who have delivered complex infrastructure across South Sudan — from residential buildings to petrol stations, roads, and bridges.

We own and operate a modern fleet of heavy machinery: excavators, bulldozers, graders, compactors, and concrete plants. This enables us to execute civil works of any size and complexity, even in challenging rural or hilly environments, without compromising quality or timelines.

  • Certified civil & structural engineers
  • Heavy equipment operators & technicians
  • Project management professionals (PMP)
  • In‑house maintenance & logistics teams
